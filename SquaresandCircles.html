Concept Behind SquaresandCircles

Inspired by various eye-catching media player graphics, this short time-based visual is an experimental piece for my electronic sketchbook of digital art.

Note: I used P3D (Processing 3D) graphics renderer for a much more pleasant web experience. OPENGL is also available and smooths out graphics automatically.
