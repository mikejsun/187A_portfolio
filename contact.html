Hi there!

Thanks for cruising by. Now that you're here, allow me to share a little description of myself and this site.

My name is Mike Sun and I'm currently studying Cognitive Science and Human-computer Interaction at the University of California, San Diego. You'll either find me experimenting with augmented reality and digital art or participating in research projects in academia dealing with user-centered design methods.

As any of my friends and family would say, I have an unrestrained sense of curiosity when it comes to topics that interest me. HCI is just another one of those things. The goal of this website is to share insightful experiences as well as things I've learned. I guess you can call it a portfolio, but I'd like to think of it as my personal diary of experiences made public.

I have high ambitions on pursuing a career under the umbrella of HCI. Most importantly, I'm always looking to learn and improve my existing ways. Please take a look around and shoot me an e-mail to let me know what you think or if you'd simply like to chat. Now please, go and have a great day!
