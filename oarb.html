OARB - Speculative Design of an Augmented Reality iPad Application

Concept Behind OARB

How influential you are in a given social atmosphere is relative to both the scenario and people around you. So far, there is no way to quantify social influence in an intuitive way, without thorough investigation of those around you. OARB is an augmented reality application that projects on people ambient orbs that represent the visual interpretation of their social influence.

With each different interaction, we must modify in our minds this hierarchy in order to know our place and properly deal with the situation given our standing. Our brains' ability to do this has governed how we come to resolution with different people within different contexts.

User Interface

The interface of OARB was designed with an exceptional user experience in mind by augmenting information onto the image of the real world and inviting users to directly manipulate the image itself. The simplicity of the design directs the users attention to the task at hand instead of worrying about the functionality of the system, creating an intuitive conversation between the human and computer.
